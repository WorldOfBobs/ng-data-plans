Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cheapest Airtel data plan in Nigeria?

The cheapest Airtel plans are shown above sorted by price. Use the filter to show plans under ₦500 or ₦1,000. The best value plans (most MB per ₦100) are highlighted with a "Best Value" badge.

How do I subscribe to Airtel data in Nigeria?

Dial *312# on your Airtel SIM to open the data menu and choose your plan. You can also buy data through the Airtel app or website. Tap any plan card above to copy the USSD code.

Do Airtel data plans expire?

Yes — all data plans have a validity period shown on each card (daily, weekly, or 30-day monthly). Unused data expires at the end of the period unless you subscribe again before expiry.

Which network has the best data in Nigeria?

Glo typically offers the most GB per naira. MTN has the widest coverage. Airtel has strong 4G in cities. 9mobile has limited coverage. Use the JollofData comparison tool to compare all carriers side by side.
